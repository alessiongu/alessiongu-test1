About

Hi! I’m a first year Multimedia Producer Way-Up at Swisscom. My expertise lies withing Photography, Videography, Editorial Design.

MY MOTIVATION

I fell in love with what the future might hold (mainly because of science fiction movies (: ) and always try to implement new ideas in what I do. My thought process usually goes: How can this be simplified and optimized? Or just: What new things are there in our world? Which usually leads me to consuming all kinds of technological trends. For example: Did you know there is a Company 3D printing fully operational rockets?!

EXPERTISE

I have experience in all basic fields of multimedia and my skills are diverse. One of my strengths lies within Editorial Design/ Layout Design where I directed and designed a 120-page fashion magazine as my maturity thesis in 2020. In that project I directed several photo shootings and was able to improve my photography, videography and sound design skills by a lot.

PERSONAL STRENGTHS

Living alone with my dad made me an independent person always open for new things. I love connecting and working with people. In my opinion working in groups enables me to advance way faster since you learn from each other’s mistakes. That’s how I try to improve.
